What is MS Project?

Microsoft Project is a project management tool that helps you plan, schedule, and track projects effectively.

Key Features

  • Task scheduling
  • Resource management
  • Progress tracking
  • Reports & charts

Why Use It?

It helps teams stay organized, meet deadlines, and manage workloads efficiently.
